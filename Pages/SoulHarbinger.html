Soul Harbinger

This is a single player game that I started back in College during my free time.

It's about a necromancer who broke open a portal to the underworld while trying to reanimate a loved one. In doing so, he unintentionally opened a path for the undead to invade the world.

Your quest is to seal the portal you created and save the people, even as they absolve you of your actions. Along the way, you slowly uncover the scars the spell left on your soul and reveal hidden truths about the world around you.

The main mechanic for this game is the ability to shift between a "Wraith" form and "Human" form. Inspired from Shadow of War's "Celebrimbor form" and "Eagles Vision" from Assassin's Creed.

Revealing hidden objects and unlocking abilities.
