Scuffed Dodgeball

This is a Multiplayer game that I am working on Using Unity's Netcode.

As the title suggests, you'll be playing a game of dodgeball with your friends, complete with special ball effects and gameplay modifiers. Unlike traditional dodgeball, players aren't eliminated by stepping out of bounds. Instead, each player has health and a limited number of stocks (lives).

Unity provides Lobby and Relay services, which I used to implement the authentication system, as well as lobby creation and joining functionality.

I am also working with SteamWorks to help publish and promote my game to a complete project.

In this project, all models and textures were generously provided by a friend, so I take no credit for the visuals. Aside from those, all backend and frontend developments including; music, GUI, and coding are created by myself.
