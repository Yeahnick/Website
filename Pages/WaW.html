From Zombies to Mapping

Discovering mod tools like Radiant introduced me to the world of level design, sparking a passion I didnâ€™t know I had. Creating a custom zombie map for Call of Duty: World at War was an incredibly fun experience, combining my love for one of my favorite game modes of all time with hands-on learning.

Through this project, I gained basic knowledge in level design, compiling, and environment creation, which further inspired my interest in game development.

Where is it now?

Unfortunately, the project was never completed and eventually abandoned. However, I did upload some of my progress with videos on YouTube. I worked on elements such as 'spawners,' 'zoning,' and progression systems like doors and wall weapons. Some of the more advanced gameplay features were beyond my skill level at the time like "Easter eggs" for exemple. Despite this, I still look back on it from time to time as a valuable learning experience that introduced me to the fundamentals of level design.
