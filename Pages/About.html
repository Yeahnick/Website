< About Me />

 Hey! I'm Yanick, a tech enthusiast and programmer from Montreal. Ever since I was a kid, I've been hooked on video games, I might as well have been born with a controller in my hands. That passion quickly turned into curiosity about how games and software work, leading me to experiment with computers at a young age. I probably spent more time modding Minecraft and tinkering with Windows XP files rather studying for my exams in elementary.

 Fast forward to today, I've studied Computer Science and gained hands-on experience in programming, game development, and web design. I love working on projects that bring creativity and technology together.

< Education />

 I studied Computer Science at Collège Gérald-Godin, completing two years in a DEC program. While I struggled with general courses, I excelled in my coding classes, so instead of letting that potential go to waste, I switched to an AEC in Web and Mobile Development at the same school. There, I earned my certificate.

 On top of that, I also completed a Game Development course on Udemy, where I learned Unity and the fundamentals of building interactive games. I'm always looking to expand my skill set, whether through self-learning, experimenting with new projects, or tackling creative coding challenges.
